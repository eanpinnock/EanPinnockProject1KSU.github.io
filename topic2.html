Mercedes-AMG GT R

Overview

The Mercedes-AMG GT R is the ultimate performance evolution of the AMG GT lineup. Engineered with track performance in mind, the GT R delivers blistering speed, precision handling, and an aggressive aerodynamic design. Nicknamed the “Beast of the Green Hell,” it was developed and tested extensively on Germany’s Nürburgring Nordschleife.

Key Features

  • 4.0L Hand-Built Twin-Turbo V8 Engine
  • 577 Horsepower and 516 lb-ft of Torque
  • Active Aerodynamics and Rear-Wheel Steering
  • Lightweight aluminum and carbon fiber construction
  • Track-tuned suspension and performance electronics
